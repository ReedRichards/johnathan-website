Services

Polished
Concrete

From new concrete to existing concrete WPC can transform ones concrete slab into a one of a kind floor. Polished concrete involves several stages of mechanically grinding your concrete floor with specialized diamond tooling. Through each stage of concrete grinding, the pores of the concrete surface gradually close resulting in a harder and shinier floor. In addition, we add a concrete impregnating densifier and sealer that soaks into the concrete, hardens and becomes part of the polished concrete floor.

Epoxy
Floor System

Epoxy floor coatings work well in business settings where high-traffic and frequent chemical and biological spills are expected. Epoxy floor systems are coatings that bond directly to the surface and offer long-term protection. Depending on the clients needs, flakes and slip-resistant materials can be added to protect your customers and employees from unwanted falls. Epoxy floors work well in warehouses, airplane hangers, commercial kitchens, factories, medical facilities, automotive shops, etc.
